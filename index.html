Why Stayci?

Stayci's Sound

"In the dynamic world of Pop/R&B, Stayci reigns supreme, drawing inspiration from the legendary voices of Beyonce and Mariah Carey, while infusing her music with the soulful essence of gospel and jazz. A native of Los Angeles, her roots run deep in the city's vibrant artistic scene, despite a childhood home that failed to nurture her creative spark. However, at 22, she embraced her true calling as a songwriter and performer, igniting stages with her magnetic presence and infectious rhythms. With a voice that evokes both the smooth timbre of a saxophone and the ethereal beauty of a butterfly, Stayci's music transcends boundaries, captivating audiences with its irresistible blend of groove and emotion, paying homage to the city that shaped her."
